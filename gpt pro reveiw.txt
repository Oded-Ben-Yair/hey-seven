**Fix (actionable):**

* Patch deterministic IDs to use the same null-byte delimiter pattern as RRF:

  * In `src/rag/pipeline.py` replace `text + str(source)` with `f"{text}\x00{source}"` before hashing (`src/rag/pipeline.py:L10-L12`). ([GitHub][8])
