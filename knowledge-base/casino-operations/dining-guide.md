## Comp Dining Policies

### How Comp Credits Work for Dining
- Comp credits are earned through gaming activity (see loyalty-programs.md for earning rates)
- Credits can be applied toward food charges at participating restaurants
- **Alcohol is typically excluded** from comp credit redemption at most properties
- **Tips/gratuity are excluded** from comp credits â€” guests pay cash or charge to room
- Some properties allow partial comp application (comp covers food, guest pays remainder + tip)

### Comp Dining Tiers
| Loyalty Tier | Typical Dining Comp | Eligible Venues |
|-------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Base/Entry | Buffet discounts only | Buffet, quick-service |
| Mid-tier | $25-50 meal comps | Casual dining, buffet |
| Premium | $50-150 meal comps | Most restaurants including signature |
| VIP/High-roller | Full dining comp | All venues including celebrity chef |

### Important Notes for Hosts
- Always confirm comp balance before promising specific dining experiences
- Celebrity chef restaurants may have comp restrictions during peak hours
- Group dining (6+) often requires manager approval for full comp
- Holiday and special event menus may have surcharges not covered by comps

## Reservation Systems

### OpenTable Integration
Both Mohegan Sun and Foxwoods use OpenTable for reservation management at fine dining venues.
- Guests can book directly via OpenTable app or website
- Loyalty tier members receive priority reservation windows (typically 48-hour advance access)
- VIP/host-managed guests can request reservations through their dedicated host

### Walk-In Availability
- Fine dining: Reservations strongly recommended, especially Friday-Saturday
- Casual dining: Walk-ins generally accommodated, 15-45 minute waits on weekends
- Quick service: No reservations needed
- Buffet: No reservations, but expect 30-60 minute waits on holiday weekends

### Peak Times
- Friday dinner: 6:00-8:30 PM (highest demand)
- Saturday dinner: 5:30-9:00 PM
- Sunday brunch: 10:00 AM-1:00 PM (buffet especially busy)
- Holiday weekends: All venues at capacity, plan 1-2 weeks ahead

## Dietary Accommodations

### Advance Notice Requirements
| Dietary Need | Recommended Notice | Available At |
|-------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Halal | 2 weeks minimum | Select restaurants with advance coordination |
| Kosher | 7 days minimum | Requires pre-arrangement with kitchen management |
| Vegan | Same day (most venues) | The Shed (vegan-focused), most restaurants offer options |
| Vegetarian | Same day | All restaurants |
| Gluten-free | Same day | Most restaurants, cross-contamination disclaimer applies |
| Nut allergy | Same day (inform server) | All restaurants can accommodate with advance notice |

### Special Dietary Notes
- **The Shed** at Mohegan Sun is the best option for plant-forward and vegan dining with a dedicated menu
- Fine dining restaurants are generally most accommodating for complex dietary needs
- Buffets label major allergens but cross-contamination risk is higher
- Hosts should proactively ask about dietary restrictions when arranging dining for VIP guests
- For large group events with dietary requirements, coordinate directly with the restaurant's private dining manager
