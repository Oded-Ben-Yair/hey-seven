# Entertainment Guide

## Mohegan Sun Arena
The Mohegan Sun Arena is a 10,000-seat world-class entertainment venue hosting major concerts, sporting events, and special performances throughout the year. Located in the Casino of the Earth, it has welcomed legendary artists including Elton John, Bruce Springsteen, and Beyoncé. The arena features premium seating options, VIP suites, and a state-of-the-art sound system.

## Wolf Den
The Wolf Den is Mohegan Sun's intimate free entertainment venue, seating approximately 350 guests. Located in the Casino of the Earth near the race book, it offers nightly performances including rock, country, pop, and tribute bands. No tickets required — seating is first-come, first-served. Shows typically begin at 8 PM on weekdays and 7 PM on weekends.

## Comix Comedy Club
Comix at Mohegan Sun is a premier comedy venue featuring nationally touring comedians and rising stars. The intimate setting provides an up-close comedy experience with a full bar and food menu. Shows run Thursday through Sunday with occasional special weeknight events. Tickets can be purchased at the Mohegan Sun Box Office or online.

## Outdoor Events & Seasonal Programming
Mohegan Sun hosts seasonal outdoor events including summer concert series, holiday celebrations, and themed festivals. The property's outdoor spaces transform for special occasions including New Year's Eve celebrations, Fourth of July fireworks, and seasonal food and wine festivals.

## Tickets & VIP Packages
Tickets for all Mohegan Sun entertainment events can be purchased through:
- The Mohegan Sun Box Office (located near the Arena entrance)
- Online at mohegansun.com/entertainment
- By phone at the box office number

VIP packages are available for select shows and include premium seating, backstage access, meet-and-greet opportunities, and complimentary food and beverage options. Momentum Rewards members receive priority access and exclusive pre-sale opportunities.

## Family-Friendly Entertainment
While Mohegan Sun is primarily an adult entertainment destination, select daytime events and seasonal programming welcome families. Kids Quest provides supervised entertainment for children during special family event days. The Shops at Mohegan Sun offer family-friendly browsing, and select restaurants welcome guests of all ages during lunch hours.
