## VIP Amenities by Loyalty Tier

### Upgrade Policies
Upgrades are based on Average Daily Theoretical (ADT) and loyalty tier status:

| Loyalty Tier | Room Upgrade Eligibility | Additional VIP Amenities |
|-------------|-------------------------|--------------------------|
| Base/Entry | Standard rate, no upgrades | None |
| Mid-tier | 10-20% discount on published rates | Late checkout request (subject to availability) |
| Premium | Complimentary standard room (based on ADT), suite upgrade waitlist | Early check-in (2 PM), late checkout (1 PM), welcome amenity |
| VIP/Host-managed | Complimentary suite (based on ADT), priority upgrade | Guaranteed early check-in/late checkout, in-room amenity package, dedicated check-in desk |

### ADT-Based Room Comp Thresholds (Industry Typical)
| ADT Range | Typical Room Comp |
|-----------|-------------------|
| $100-250 | Discounted rate (50-75% off) |
| $250-500 | Complimentary standard room |
| $500-1,000 | Complimentary premium room or standard suite |
| $1,000-2,500 | Complimentary suite |
| $2,500+ | Premium suite, VIP amenity package |

### Early Check-In and Late Checkout
- **Standard guests**: Check-in 4:00 PM, checkout 11:00 AM
- **Mid-tier loyalty**: Late checkout request honored when available (typically 12:00-1:00 PM)
- **Premium loyalty**: Early check-in 2:00 PM, late checkout 1:00 PM (priority)
- **VIP/Host-managed**: Flexible check-in/checkout, guaranteed within reason (not during sold-out events)

### In-Room VIP Amenities
- **Welcome amenity**: Fruit plate, chocolates, or wine (for premium tier and above)
- **Turndown service**: Available nightly for suites and VIP rooms
- **Premium bath products**: Upgraded products in suite-level rooms
- **Minibar**: Complimentary stocking for VIP guests (host-arranged)

## Resort Fee Structure

### Mohegan Sun
- Resort fee: $25-35/night (varies by tower and room type)
- Includes: Wi-Fi, fitness center access, pool access, local calls
- **Loyalty waiver**: Ascend and Soar tier members receive resort fee waiver
- Resort fees are charged even on complimentary room nights (important to communicate to guests)

### Foxwoods
- Resort fee: $20-30/night (varies by hotel and room type)
- Includes: Wi-Fi, fitness center access, parking
- **Loyalty waiver**: Diamond tier members receive resort fee waiver
- Fox Tower may have different fee structure due to non-smoking premium

### Important Notes for Hosts
- Always disclose resort fees proactively, especially for comped stays
- "Complimentary room" guests still pay resort fee unless loyalty tier qualifies for waiver
- Group bookings may negotiate resort fee inclusion
- Holiday and event weekends may have modified resort fee structures
- Some properties waive resort fees as a goodwill gesture for service recovery situations

## Booking and Reservation Notes

### Direct Booking vs. Third-Party
- Loyalty program benefits only apply to direct bookings (property website, phone, host-arranged)
- Third-party bookings (Expedia, Booking.com) do not earn tier credits or qualify for comps
- Hosts should always encourage direct booking for guest benefit

### Group Blocks
- 10+ rooms: Contact group sales for negotiated rates
- Casino host can facilitate group blocks for VIP guest events (birthday parties, corporate retreats)
- Room block attrition: typically 80% pickup required to avoid penalties

### Cancellation Policies
- Standard: 48-72 hours before arrival (varies by rate type)
- Comped rooms: 24-48 hours, but repeated no-shows affect future comp eligibility
- Non-refundable rates: Available at 15-25% discount, no cancellation permitted
- Event weekends: May require full prepayment with stricter cancellation terms
