# Momentum Rewards Program — Mohegan Sun

## Momentum Rewards Program Overview

Mohegan Sun's loyalty program. Guests earn Momentum Points and Tier Credits by playing slots, table games, and poker, as well as through hotel stays, dining, and entertainment purchases. Points can be redeemed for free play, dining, hotel stays, and retail purchases throughout the resort. Free to join at any Momentum Desk or kiosk on the gaming floor, online at mohegansun.com, or via the Mohegan Sun app. Card also works at Mohegan Sun Pocono. Momentum Desk is open 24/7 on the gaming floor.

## Momentum Tier: Core

Entry-level tier for all new Momentum members. Earn Momentum Points and Tier Credits on all gaming and resort spending. Access to member-only promotions and the Mohegan Sun app for account management. No minimum play required. Upgrade to Ignite tier by earning 2,500 Tier Credits in a semi-annual period.

## Momentum Tier: Ignite

Mid-level Momentum status earned at 2,500 Tier Credits per semi-annual period. Enhanced rewards earning rate. Select event invitations. Dining discounts at participating restaurants. Birthday bonus offer. Benefits include enhanced multiplier days and quarterly reload offers.

## Momentum Tier: Leap

Premium Momentum tier earned at 10,000 Tier Credits per semi-annual period. Priority access to entertainment pre-sales. Enhanced earning rates on all play. Exclusive event invitations. Priority check-in at hotel. Enhanced room rate discounts. Valet discounts at resort entrances.

## Momentum Tier: Ascend

Elite Momentum tier earned at 25,000 Tier Credits per semi-annual period. Premium benefits including complimentary valet parking. Room upgrades when available. Dedicated casino host assignment. Priority restaurant reservations. Invitation-only VIP events. Significantly enhanced earn rates on all play.

## Momentum Tier: Soar

Highest Momentum tier, available by invitation only based on sustained high-level play. Full VIP treatment with personalized service. Exclusive access to premium events and amenities. Personal casino host available for concierge-level service. Complimentary suite upgrades when available. Maximum earn rates on all play. Priority everything across the resort.

## Momentum Points Redemption

Momentum Points can be redeemed at any participating restaurant, the hotel front desk, spa, retail shops, and the entertainment box office throughout Mohegan Sun. Redeem at any cashier, restaurant host stand, or retail register by presenting your Momentum card. Points are also redeemable for free play at slot machines and kiosks. Check your balance at any Momentum Desk, kiosk, or through the Mohegan Sun app.

## Current Promotions

Check the Momentum Desk or mohegansun.com/promotions for current seasonal offers, multiplier days, and special events. Promotions change regularly. Common promotions include Double Momentum Points days, new member bonus offers, seasonal gift giveaways, and slot tournament entries. Sign up for email alerts through the Mohegan Sun app to never miss an offer.

## Tier Reset Schedule

Momentum tiers operate on a semi-annual reset schedule. Players must re-qualify each six-month period based on Tier Credits earned. Status is valid through the end of the qualification period. This creates urgency for consistent play but guests should be aware of the reset timeline.
